1:1 LESSONS

Stu's primary instrument is the guitar. He also teaches music production, songwriting, improvisation, music theory, ear training, and ukulele to students of all ages.

Choose from a 30-minute session ($40), a 45-minute session ($60), or a 60-minute session ($80) and receive personalized guidance, feedback, and new practice material each week.

Plus, refer a client and get a free session.

Stu Pender, Chicago-born musician and recording artist, weaves elements of jazz, ambience, minimalism, and pop songwriting into orchestrations for the guitar, voice, and laptop.
