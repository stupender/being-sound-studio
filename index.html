BESPOKE SONIC WORLDS

Craft your sonic world.

I work with artists, visionary founders, and cultural builders to craft bespoke sonic worlds — original music, sound identities, and long-form compositions that embody a deeper creative vision.

I approach this work as sonic worldbuilding: creating a living sound language that carries meaning, memory, and values over time. Sound that humanizes, gathers, and supports shared presence.

Drawing from practices of contemplative listening, music for transition and threshold, and the deep roots of rhythm across cultures, I help shape sound that feels alive. Sound oriented toward presence over noise, coherence over trend, and connection over distraction.

If you’re building something meant to last — a project, a company, a community — sound matters more than you think.

WHAT THIS CAN SUPPORT

  • Podcasts, courses, and long-form audio experiences
  • Meditation, somatic, or therapeutic practices
  • Brands and platforms seeking a coherent sonic identity
  • Creative projects that value continuity, presence, and resonance

Each project is shaped to meet its specific use and audience.

WHAT YOU RECEIVE

Every collaboration is unique, and may include:

  • One original sonic composition that serves as your core sonic identity
  • Variations or extensions for different contexts or durations, while maintaining a cohesive sonic identity
  • Thoughtful integration with voice, silence, and pacing
  • Clean, ready-to-publish audio files
  • Optional ongoing collaboration (mixing, extensions, monthly support)

You own the rights to use the work within your platform and offerings.

INVESTMENT

Projects typically range from $2,000-$4,000, depending on scope and duration.

Ongoing or extended collaborations are discussed case by case.

IS THIS FOR YOU?

This work is for people who:

  • Are attentive to how sound shapes attention, emotion, and nervous system state
  • Want a sonic identity that feels intentional
  • Value craft, subtlety, and long-term coherence
  • Are drawn to work made with care, depth, and integrity

ABOUT BEING SOUND

Being Sound is a sonic arts studio founded by Stu Pender, crafting sonic worlds for artists and cultural builders while cultivating spaces where people gather to listen, learn, and practice together.

ABOUT STU PENDER

Stu Pender, Chicago-born musician and sonic artist, weaves elements of improvisation, songwriting, and electronics into orchestrations for the guitar, voice, and laptop.

A founding member of the hip-hop collective Poetic Thrust, Stu has also worked various roles in the studio with artists Yoh, Cartoons, Topaz Jones, Carlos Niño and Laraaji. Sharing the stage with artists August Ponthier, Photay, Elijah Wolf and Maggie Rogers, he's showcased his talents as an internationally touring guitarist, at The MoMA PS1, United Nations, The Beacon Theatre, and many more.

Stu’s debut full length album, Stillness In Motion was released in 2021 on the Youngbloods label, earning acclaim from Los Angeles’ KCRW and Apple Music.

Stu graduated with a Bachelor of Arts in Jazz Studies from SUNY Purchase in 2015. He completed a certificate training for sound in the healing arts with the New York Open Center's Sound & Integrative Music Institute in 2022.

He currently resides in Brooklyn, New York.
