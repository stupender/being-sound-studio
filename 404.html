    BESPOKE SOUND

    Imagine your sonic world.

    You've poured intention into every detail of your work — the words, the visuals, the physical space. But an immersive experience is instantly broken by auditory dissonance.

    I create bespoke soundscapes for people whose work demands the same level of mastery in its audio as they bring to their craft. Sound that doesn't just accompany your work, but holds it.

    Every piece begins with listening: to your work, to its resonance, and to what's being communicated beyond the words. The music is created from an immersion into this world.

    WHAT THIS CAN SUPPORT

    • Podcasts, courses, and memberships
    • Meditation and somatic practices that require functional, environmental audio to regulate the nervous system and deepen focus
    • Brands, platforms, and creative projects that need a coherent sonic identity
    • Physical spaces, retreats, and installations where sound acts as sonic architecture, shaping the movement and mood of the room

    Each project is shaped to fit its specific use, audience, and context.

    HOW WE WORK TOGETHER

    Every project begins with a conversation about your work, your audience, and the kind of attention the sound needs to hold.

    From there, projects take different shapes:

    • A single soundscape for a class, meditation, podcast, or specific moment
    • A series of pieces for a course, program, or body of recordings
    • A full sonic identity, with a theme, variations, and transitions
    • Custom sound for a space, composed to support the room itself
    • Live sound for retreats and gatherings

    WHAT YOU RECEIVE

    Every collaboration is tailored, but may include:

    • One or more original compositions
    • Variations or extensions for different contexts and durations
    • Thoughtful integration with voice, silence, and pacing
    • Clean, ready-to-publish audio files in professional formats
    • Ownership for use across your platform and offerings
    • Optional ongoing collaboration for mixing, extensions, or regular support

    PRICING

    What a project costs depends on the kind of sound it asks for.

    Environmental & Atmospheric ($400+)
    This is functional sound design. Drones, tones, field recordings, and ambient textures meant to act as auditory architecture. Like a wash of color on a wall, these soundscapes create a physical space or state of mind without demanding the listener's direct attention. Ideal for meditations, treatment rooms, or deep-focus practices.

    Composed & Structural ($1,500 – $4,000)
    This is narrative sound. Melody, arrangement, and structure that develop into a clear, recognizable sonic signature. This work guides the listener through a journey and defines your auditory brand identity. Ideal for podcast themes, course identities, and cohesive brand worlds.

    Larger commissions — retreats, platforms, or systems of pieces working together — are scoped on their own.

    The discovery call is where we find what your work is actually asking for. It's free, with no expectation beyond seeing if the work fits.

    IS THIS FOR YOU?

    This work is most aligned with people who:

    • Have a clear sense of what you're making — even if not what it sounds like yet
    • Have invested in their craft, and understand the difference between curated stock audio and a bespoke sonic identity
    • Can give the work the time it needs, rather than needing music tomorrow
    • Want sound that acts as an environment, rather than an intrusion
    • Value deep listening and want music that is genuinely embodied — composed by a human with a complete musical sensibility, not assembled from algorithms or generated on demand

    MUSIC LESSONS

    Develop your musical craft.

    Music Lessons are a 1:1 space for people who want to grow their musicianship while staying connected to intuition, integrity, and expression.

    Whether you're picking up an instrument for the first time, or returning to one you've played for years, we begin with the songs and sounds that already inspire you, using them as the doorway into embodied craft.

    AREAS OF FOCUS

    • Musical craft and theory
    • Instrumental technique and fluency
    • Building a personal repertoire or songbook
    • Deep listening, ear training, and the body's role in sound
    • Improvisation and songwriting
    • Music technology, recording, and production tools

    WHO THIS IS FOR

    Students of all ages and skill levels, from complete beginners to working musicians refining their craft. My primary instrument is the guitar. I also teach ukulele, voice, and music technology.

    PRICING

    $100 per hour. Packages, sliding scale, and alternate payment options available upon request.

    ABOUT BEING SOUND

    Being Sound is a sonic arts studio by Stu Pender. Exploring sound as practice — from deep listening to sounding with.

    ABOUT STU PENDER

    Stu Pender is a Chicago-based guitarist, composer, and sonic artist whose work is rooted in deep listening and sounding with.

    A founding member of the hip-hop collective Poetic Thrust, Stu has performed and recorded with artists including Photay, Carlos Niño and Laraaji, Topaz Jones, Elijah Wolf, and Maggie Rogers — touring internationally and performing at the Greek Theatre, MoMA PS1, Central Park Summerstage, and the Beacon Theatre. His solo recordings have been featured on KCRW, KEXP, and Apple Music's Headspace playlist.

    Stu's practice is shaped by years of study across traditions — from jazz (John Abercrombie, Barry Harris, SUNY Purchase) to West African kora music (Derek Gripper) to North Indian raga (Silvia Nakkach) — alongside contemplative training in the Zen and Tibetan Bön traditions. He holds a certificate in sound and the healing arts from the New York Open Center.

    Through Being Sound, he composes original music and soundscapes for practitioners, creators, and spaces, and teaches guitar and music privately. He lives in Edgewater, Chicago.
